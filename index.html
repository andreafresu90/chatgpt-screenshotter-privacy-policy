Privacy Policy for ChatGPT Screenshotter

This privacy policy applies to the ChatGPT Screenshotter Google Chrome extension (the "extension").

The extension is designed to allow users to take screenshots of chat conversations with ChatGPT.The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The extension only processes the chat conversations that the user chooses to screenshot, and the resulting screenshots are only stored locally on the user's device. The extension does not transmit the screenshots or any other data to any external servers or third parties.

The extension does not collect any personal data from the user or from the websites that the user visits. The extension does not access or track any browsing history, search queries, or other personal data.

We take the privacy of our users seriously and have designed the extension to minimize the collection and processing of personal data. If you have any questions or concerns about the privacy practices of the extension, please contact us at andreafresu90@gmail.com.
